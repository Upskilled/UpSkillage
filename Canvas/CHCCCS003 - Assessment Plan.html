ARE YOU READY TO BE ASSESSED?

Welcome to the Student Assessment Tasks for CHCCCS003 Increase The Safety Of Individuals At Risk Of Suicide. These tasks have been designed to help you demonstrate the skills and knowledge that you have learnt during your course. Please ensure that you read the instructions provided with these tasks carefully. The Student User Guide provides important information for you relating to completing assessment successfully.

Prior to starting your assessment, familiarise yourself with the Assessments overview section which can be found in the 101Start Course in your MyUpskilled. The information contained in this document is relevant to all assessments you undertake.

You are required to complete two assessment activities for CHCCCS003

  1. CHCCCS003 - Knowledge Quiz
  2. CHCCCS003 - Practical Task

 

Grading outcomes

  • The outcome of an assessment tasks as either Satisfactory (S) or Not Satisfactory (NS)
  • When all assessment tasks are deemed Satisfactory (S), the unit outcome is marked Competent (C)
  • If at least one of the assessment tasks is deemed Not Satisfactory (NS), the unit outcome is Not Yet Competent (NYC)

 

Student declaration

 

Access and complete your student declaration before you begin your assessment tasks.

 

Knowledge Quiz

Assessment task one is a knowledge quiz. You must answer all questions correctly.

 

Practical Task

 

Assessment task two is a practical task. You must complete all requirements of the assessment.
