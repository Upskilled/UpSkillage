Welcome from the Upskilled CHC Faculty

A warm welcome to you from the Upskilled CHC Faculty. Upskilled is dedicated to ensuring that you receive ongoing support and relevant information throughout your student journey. The Upskilled learning environment is an interactive one, enabling you to have ongoing opportunities to collaborate with other students, join online group support sessions and quality support from your Trainer and Assessor.

When you have completed your current qualification you may have the opportunity to receive Credit Transfers for the Units you have completed including reducing the cost and duration of other courses. If you are interested in the courses offered by Upskilled CHC Faculty please feel free to find them here: https://www.upskilled.edu.au/courses/community-services

Feel free to contact our Sales Team if you are interested in completing any of these courses in the future.

So you've chosen the diploma of community services

The health and community services sector is Australia’s second-largest employing industry – and growing. It delivers a range of medical, residential and social assistance services to support us all, and it is also responsible for the well-being of our communities. New models of care and technological advances are changing the way we work, and Australian businesses are seeking to employ skilled professionals to keep pace. This is a rich and rewarding industry to be involved in, and you’ll be amazed at the career choices available to you.

Connect

Find a Study Buddy

This is area provides an opportunity for you to connect with other students in your course so that you can support one another and engage in collaborative studies. You can create and join study groups with other students to discuss assessment requirements, practice role-plays for assessments as well as find volunteers to play the part of your volunteers for the practical assessments in your course (role-plays) etc.

Student Lounge

The Student Lounge is a forum where you can chat with other students. You can use this opportunity to ask questions and discuss different aspects of your course with other students.

Zoom - Speak face to face with other students in Zoom

You can use this to create video links with other students. This is particularly helpful for discussing practical assessments and practising role plays with other students. You can also use this tool for facilitating and recording your required practical assessments in the simulated business environment (Role Plays). You might want to share the Zoom invite link (copy and paste) in your study groups to facilitate this.
