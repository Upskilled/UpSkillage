Course calendar: book your trainer, course events and webinars

To book a time with your trainer, follow the steps below:

  1. Go to the course home calendar (calendar and link provided below).
  2. Find an open appointment booking slot with the trainer needed.
  3. Please join the meeting via the provided Zoom link at the nominated time.
  4. Notify your trainer 24hrs in Advance if you are not able to attend.

If you need help with your calendar, check out this official Canvas calendar guide.
