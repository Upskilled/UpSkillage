ARE YOU READY TO BE ASSESSED?

Welcome to the Student Assessment Tasks for Module 1 of the Diploma of Leadership and Management. These tasks have been designed to help you demonstrate the skills and knowledge that you have learnt during your course. Please ensure that you read the instructions provided with these tasks carefully. The Student User Guide provides important information for you relating to completing assessment successfully.

Familiarise yourself with the 'Assessment Guide' document that is provided in your MyUpskilled site. The information contained in this document is relevant to all assessments you undertake.

You are required to complete all assessment tasks to complete this module.

Grading outcomes

  • The outcome of an assessment tasks as either Satisfactory (S) or Not Satisfactory (NS)
  • When all assessment tasks are deemed Satisfactory (S), the unit outcome is marked Competent (C)
  • If at least one of the assessment tasks is deemed Not Satisfactory (NS), the unit outcome is Not Yet Competent (NYC)
