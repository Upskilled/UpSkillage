Assessment 1 - Third party workplace observation

The Third-Party Workplace Observation is required to be completed by an 'Observer' in the Learner’s workplace. This could be a Manager or Supervisor.

The Third-Party is required to observe and confirm that the Learner has demonstrated competence of the listed foundation skills by having completed practical tasks in the workplace.
