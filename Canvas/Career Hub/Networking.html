Building your network

Networking is when you meet with someone to discuss professional topics and hope to begin a mutually beneficial relationship. Have you ever heard the expression "it's not about what you know but who you know"? Well that's networking, and it's absolutely true in the context of a job search. Having someone within the company vouching for you can make all the difference in getting your resume in the "yes" pile and can even open up opportunities that aren't being advertised.
