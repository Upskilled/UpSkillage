Understanding Project Management

Different firms will have different names for similar positions within project management. To keep things simple we’ve broken out the project management career path into the progression from Project Coordinator to Project Manager and ultimately to Project Director. The purpose of this section is to answer frequently asked questions and give you an idea of how your career could progress in project management.

*All information on this page has been complied using figures from onetonline.org, au.talent.com and the Australian government's labourmarketinsights.gov.au - all figures are indications only, and are accurate at time of publication. Please do your own research before selecting a course or career.
