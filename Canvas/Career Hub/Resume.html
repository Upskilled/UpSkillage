Building out your resume

Below is a guide for how to build and optimise your resume. At the bottom of the page you will find a resume template and further information for you to explore.

Resume overview

The objective of your resume is to convince the hiring manager to select you for an interview. You should customise every resume you send out so that it highlights your skills which are relevant to the job you are applying for. Keep in mind that you probably only have 20-30 seconds to catch the eye of the recruiter so it is very important to make sure the structure of your resume makes your key achievements pop out.

Applicant tracking systems

Applicant Tracking Systems are programs used by over 50% of companies (98% of the ASX 100) to screen applications and resumes. These programs look through resumes and tell the company who they should interview, disqualifying over 70% of applications before they are ever read by a person. It is important to know this before sending in a resume, so that you can optimize your resume to get past the ATS.
