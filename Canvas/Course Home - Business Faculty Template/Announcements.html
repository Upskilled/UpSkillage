Announcements

Announcements allow trainers to communicate with you about course activities and where they will post interesting course-related information.

Upskilled trainers will post regular “educational soundbytes” relevant to your learning via these Announcements.

Announcements are pinned at the top of your course page so you can see the latest news relevant to your learning.
