LinkedIn Learning is a prominent online learning platform that allows Upskilled students to pursue business, software, technology and creative skills to achieve their personal and professional goals. You will gain access to the video library of engaging, specialised courses taught by recognised industry experts. LinkedIn Learning provides training to thousands of Australians through comprehensive video packages that include easy to follow courses.

You will come across a range of LinkedIn Learning videos embedded into your course, however, as an Upskilled learner, you have access to the LinkedIn Learning Business library. The Business library is a better business school for professionals. It teaches you how to manage time and projects; be more productive with business software and technology; and lead yourself, others, and the business across functions and over the span of your career. So don't let our choice of videos limit your research, head to LinkedIn Learning to explore 1000s of different videos for your reference.
