Role plays

Why do we have Role Plays?

For a student to be assessed as competent in every unit, a Registered Training Organisation like Upskilled must ensure you have absorbed the knowledge AND developed the skills.

We need to collect evidence that you can combine the knowledge and skills to demonstrate:

  • Ability to perform relevant tasks in a variety of workplace situations demonstrating consistent performance and consistent ability.
  • Understanding of what you are doing and why, when performing tasks.
  • Ability to integrate performance with understanding; to show you are able to adapt to different contexts and environments.

Role play demonstrations and presentations enable us to determine your competency and are necessary part of studying vocational education in Australia. Nobody seems to like them but put it this way, it's an opportunity to demonstrate skills you will need in the workplace, it's like a practice run only this time you get feedback from your trainer to ensure you develop the skills needed to be successful in your job.
