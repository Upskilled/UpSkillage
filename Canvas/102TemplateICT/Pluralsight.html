Pluralsight

Pluralsight is an on-demand training environment for developers, IT professionals and designers. You will see a range of Pluralsight content in your courses, however as an Upskilled Learner will gain access to a core library featuring 2,500+ popular courses on in-demand topics like cloud, data, security, software development and infrastructure. The expended library gives you access to over 7000+ courses including advanced topics to build deep expertise.

  • Expert-authored and led courses
  • Skill Assessments
  • Vendor Certification Prep
  • Role IQ
  • Hands-on Learning Projects and Labs
  • and much more
