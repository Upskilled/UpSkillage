Orientation

Orientation sessions are held fortnightly on Wednesdays at 12:30pm.

During this webinar (duration 20- 25 minutes), you will meet Upskilled team members to gain information such as how to navigate the MyUpskilled (your Learning Management System) and how to communicate with your trainer and fellow students. You will also be given tips on how to become a successful online student and what tools are available to you.

Register your attendance for our next Orientation Webinar at the Zoom registration page.
