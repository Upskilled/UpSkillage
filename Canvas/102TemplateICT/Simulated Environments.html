Simulated environments

Here at Upskilled, we have developed several simulated businesses to help contextualise our learning and assessments. 

  • Skillage IT
    • Skillage IT is our simulated IT business providing information technology solutions for small to medium-sized clients.
  • Talon Textile Fasteners
    • Talon Textile Fasteners is our simulated manufacturing business often representing the client in assessment scenarios.
  • Springy Things R Us
    • Spring Things R Us is our simulated supply business, supplying the raw materials to Talon Textile Fasteners.
