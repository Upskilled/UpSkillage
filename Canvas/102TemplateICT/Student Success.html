Student Success

Our Upskilled Student Success team is here to support you throughout your course. Contact us to discuss any non-academic or technical queries that you have. This includes any enquiries you might have outside of the learning and assessment content, such as:

  • Course extensions (different to assessment extension which you will need to discuss with your Trainer)
  • MyUpskilled learning platform navigational assistance
  • Training plans
  • Administration queries

You can contact us via phone or email. Please allow up to 2 business days for us to respond

Call: 1300 009 924 (Office Hours)

Email: studentsupport@upskilled.edu.au

We look forward to travelling with you throughout your journey.
