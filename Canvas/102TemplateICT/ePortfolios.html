ePortfolios

ePortfolios are a place where you can display and discuss the significant learning experiences that are happening during your learning process. You can use an ePortfolio to:

  • Create an online educational journal for reflection.
  • Create an online site that can be turned in as an online Assignment.
  • Demonstrate mastery of course Outcomes.
  • Share your best work from multiple courses.
  • Showcase professional-quality work for prospective employers.
  • They are tied to your account, not to a specific course, which allows you to create multiple ePortfolios and to choose how you share them. ePortfolios can be set to allow private or public access.
