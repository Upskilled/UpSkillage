Turnitin Similarity Checking

Turnitin is a text-matching software that identifies either similar or exact matches between submitted work and other digital material. This material may include the Internet, electronic databases and other students' work.

Where can I check my draft assignments?

If you are unsure whether or not your written work may too closely resemble your source material, you may wish to check your DRAFT assignment through text-matching software before submitting the final version of your assignment. The University provides the Turnitin Originality Check course in MyUni for you to check text-matching in your draft assignments.

A report is generated which flags instances where your writing is similar or matches one of the sources. In particular, you can use this report to make sure that you have used quotes and referenced correctly.

Using the report you can review and revise your writing before finally submitting your assignment.

How to use the MyUni Turnitin Originality Check

  1. Join or log in to the MyUni Turnitin Originality Check course.
  2. Select the green button on the right to Go straight to uploading your draft assignment.
  3. On the next page, select the Upload submission button to upload your submission.
  4. Follow the instructions to submit a paper.

How to Submit/Resubmit Another Assignment

  1. Log in to the MyUni Turnitin Originality Check course used above.
  2. Select the green button on the right to Go straight to uploading your draft assignment.
  3. For the current submitted assignment, Go to the 3 vertical dots, then select "Resubmit paper" from the drop down menu.
  4. Follow the instructions to submit a paper.

Note: After 3 resubmissions, a new Similarity Report will take 24 hours to generate.

Where should I complete my final assignment submission?

Please remember that all final assignment submissions must occur through your MyUni course.

How to ensure success when submitting assignments through Turnitin

If you are required to submit an assignment through Turnitin please remember the following:

  • Be aware of the file type expected in the submission.
  • If you are planning to check your work via the MyUni Turnitin Originality Check please do so a few days before the assignment due date. This will allow time for you to view the similarity report (Links to an external site.) and revise and resubmit your work accordingly.

How do I access a receipt for my Turnitin assignment submission?

To download a digital receipt with details about your submission, click the Document icon [3].

How can I check any feedback provided by my marker?

If your marker has provided written feedback, this can be accessed via MyUni. See the accessing feedback page for further information.
