Zoom - Speak face to face with other students in Zoom

You can use this to create video links with other students. This is particularly helpful for discussing practical assessments and practicing role plays with other students. You can also use this tool for facilitating and recording your required practical assessments in the simulated business environment (Role Plays). 

 

Record your role-plays: Zoom video sessions including recording for assessments

There is a Zoom section on the Home Page of your course where you can download your own private Zoom account, that is if you don't already have an account. Zoom is a great tool to capture the recorded role-plays required as assessment submissions in your course. You should use your own private Zoom account if you wish to 'record' a meeting with other students. This can be used when you are recording a practical assessment such as a role-play, for that purpose the recording needs to be saved to your computer so that it can be uploaded as an assessment submission when required.
