Ask me anything sessions

Ask Me Anything sessions are facilitated by a range of fully qualified Upskilled IT Trainers. This is where you can seek assistance on your learning and assessment contents and literally ask the trainers anything. The times and days these are held will be announced each week by your trainer via Announcements

  • Look out for regular Announcements from your trainer/s notifying you of when these are held and how to register. Watch our Forums and Announcements video to ensure you are set up to receive these important messages.
  • Alternatively, go to the Course Calendar to register your attendance at the next Ask Me Anything session with multiple time slots available each week.
  • You can also post questions in the Ask Me Anything discussion forum where you post questions about the course content, activities or assessment. If you see a question posted by another student and you know the answer, feel free to reply.
